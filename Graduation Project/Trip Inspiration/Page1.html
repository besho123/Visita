Relax in Luxury Resorts of the Red Sea Riviera

Laura Farber, President of the 2020 Tournament of Roses, shares her
favorite places for local and visiting shoppers.

Why Visit?

  • Visiting luxury resorts on the Red Sea Riviera is an exceptional experience, blending natural beauty, world-class amenities, and ultimate relaxation.
  • Explore top tourist resorts and enjoy the scenic views.

Top Experiences to Try

1. Diving & Snorkeling
  • The Red Sea is one of the world's top diving and snorkeling destinations, known for its crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and diverse marine life. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced diver, the Red Sea offers unforgettable underwater experiences.
2. Luxury Yacht Cruise
  • Exploring the Red Sea aboard a luxury yacht offers an unparalleled experience,
    • combining opulent comfort with the region's rich history and natural beauty. From the vibrant marine life to ancient cultural sites,a yacht cruise in this area promises both relaxation and adventure.
3. Private Spa Treatments
  • Indulge in a sea-view spa session at your resort for ultimate relaxation.
4. Visit Giftun Island (Hurghada)
  • A pristine island with white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and excellent
    • snorkeling spots.
5. Parasailing & Water Sports
  • The Red Sea is renowned for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine
    • life, making it an ideal destination for parasailing and various water sports. Popular locations such as Hurghada and Sharm El Sheikh offer a wide range of activities to suit all adventure enthusiasts. Parasailing provides a unique opportunity to soar above the sea, enjoying panoramic views of the coastline In Hurghada.
